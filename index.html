- About Us -

The Bear Lake Senior Center is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit charitable organization. Everyone, of any age, is welcome at the Center. We are open to any suggestions on how to improve our Center. Board meetings are held the first Tuesday of every month. Stop by the front office for board member contact information. The Board members, and all of our volunteers, put their hearts and souls into the Center.

☼ A place for the community to come together. ☼


- Mission Statement -

To provide a nutritious noon meal and home-delivered meals to seniors who are unable, either physically or financially, to provide for themselves. To enrich the daily lives of seniors by providing a creative environment that stimulates them physically, mentally, socially, and nutritionally. To provide seniors the opportunity to live the best life possible by including a wide variety of programs, activities, and classes, as well as special events.

☼ Changing lives one meal at a time. ☼
